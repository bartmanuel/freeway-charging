Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

LetsJustDrive does not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive data. Period.

Location

The app asks for your device's location for one purpose only: to know where you are so it can show you charging stations along your route and track how far away each station is as you drive. Your location is used entirely on-device and in memory — it is never sent to our servers, never stored, and never shared with any third party.

What we do not collect

  • No account, name, email address, or any form of identity
  • No location history or trip logs
  • No analytics, usage events, or crash reports
  • No advertising identifiers or tracking cookies
  • No payment or financial information

Network requests

When you search for a route, the destination you type is sent to Google's routing API solely to compute directions. Your current GPS position is not included in that request. No other data leaves your device.

Third-party services

The app uses Google Maps for route calculation and map display. Google's own privacy policy governs how they handle routing requests. We do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a new date at the top.

Contact

Questions? Reach us at privacy@letsjustdrive.app.
